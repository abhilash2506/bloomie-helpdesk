# Bloomie Product Intro

## What Bloomie Is
Bloomie is a private, role-based HR helpdesk platform built to help organizations centralize employee support.

It combines:
- smart guided answers from approved company knowledge
- ticketing and tracking
- employee self-service
- reusable community discussions
- role-based admin control

## Why It Matters
Most HR teams are overwhelmed by repetitive questions that should not become manual back-and-forth work every day.

Bloomie reduces that noise by helping employees:
- get answers faster
- raise the right request
- track progress in one place

At the same time, it helps admins:
- manage support operations
- keep policy answers consistent
- surface recurring issues before they grow

## Who It Is For
- hotel groups
- retail chains
- healthcare networks
- education groups
- enterprise HR teams
- multi-location organizations
- service providers managing multiple client brands

## Role Model
- `Master Admin`: founder/backend owner with top-level control
- `Admin`: support operators assigned by Master Admin
- `User`: employees using the helpdesk for support and referrals

## Core Experience
- Raise a Ticket
- Track Ticket
- Ask Bloomie
- Knowledge Base
- Community Forum
- POSH / Safety
- Employee Referral
- My Recent Tickets

## Bloomie Advantage
- private and organization-controlled
- HR-first instead of generic service desk first
- multilingual setup
- ticket + knowledge + community in one flow
- scalable from smaller teams to large organizations when backed by production architecture

## Positioning
Bloomie should be positioned as:

`A private employee support operating system for HR teams`

or

`A local-first smart HR helpdesk with trusted answers and case management`
