# Bloomie Marketing Deck

## Slide 1. Title
Bloomie  
Private Employee Support Platform for HR Teams

## Slide 2. The Problem
- HR teams spend too much time on repetitive employee queries
- support is fragmented across email, calls, chat, and spreadsheets
- policies are hard to find and inconsistently communicated
- sensitive issues need tracked, confidential workflows
- leadership lacks visibility into recurring support pain points

## Slide 3. The Cost of Not Fixing It
- slow employee response times
- lower trust in HR operations
- repeated payroll, leave, onboarding, and referral queries
- missed SLAs and poor escalation discipline
- growing support workload as headcount increases

## Slide 4. The Solution
Bloomie gives organizations one branded internal support portal for:
- guided answers
- tickets and tracking
- knowledge base
- community forum
- POSH/safety routing
- employee referral workflows

## Slide 5. How Bloomie Works
1. Employee asks Bloomie or searches knowledge
2. Bloomie responds using approved company sources
3. Reusable issues stay in KB or forum
4. Sensitive or unresolved issues become tracked tickets
5. Admin manages SLA, response, and reporting
